How to get here?

By Taxi: Take the taxi to S14,  3 Science Drive 2, National University of Singapore, Lower Kent Ridge Road. It takes about 30-45 minutes, S$25-$30 from Changi airport, or 15-25 minutes, S$15-$20 from city-centre.

By MRT:  The nearest MRT station is Kent Ridge MRT Station, Lower Kent Ridge Road(CC24, Circle Line). Transfer to the SBS Transit Bus service 95 or the free Internal Shuttle Bus service A1, D2, at the bus stop outside Kent Ridge MRT Station. Alight at the second bus stop at LT27 and walk down the road for 50 meters.

About Faculty of Science

The Faculty of Science is well endowed with world renowned award-winning professors who are passionate about teaching and research. There are state-of-the-art laboratories and campus-wide virtual network learning environment. To find out more about the NUS Faculty of Science: http://www.science.nus.edu.sg/
