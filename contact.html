How to get here?

By Taxi: Take the taxi to Tahir Foundation Building, MD 1, National University of Singapore, 12 Science Drive 2. It takes about 30-45 minutes, S$25-$30 from Changi airport, or 15-25 minutes, S$15-$20 from city-centre.

By MRT:  The nearest MRT station is Kent Ridge MRT Station (CC24, Circle Line).

About Faculty of Science

The Faculty of Science is well endowed with world renowned award-winning professors who are passionate about teaching and research. There are state-of-the-art laboratories and campus-wide virtual network learning environment. To find out more about the NUS Faculty of Science: http://www.science.nus.edu.sg/
